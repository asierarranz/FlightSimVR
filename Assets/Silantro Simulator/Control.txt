Shoot - Left Ctrl
Pitch - Up and Down
Roll - Left and Right
Flap - Press down Alpha 3(increase flap angle) and Alpha 4(reduce flap angle) (Number keys)
Rudder - (<)(steer left and rudder left) and Greater than(>)(steer right and rudder right)
Afterburner - f12 (Function key)
Manual Destroy - f5 (Function key)
//
//
Others can be selected from the Control Board Prefab in the scene
